My research interests include Motion Planning, Learning-Based SLAM and sensor-fusion techniques, Advanced Control, and Modeling and Simulation. My academic and professional journey has provided me with extensive hands-on experience with ground robots, autonomous vehicles, mobile manipulators and autonomous underwater vehicles. Before UCSD, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Bombay in 2023, where I dived deeper into Systems and Control engineering -- a journey that naturally evolved into my pursuit of Robotics.

I enjoy tinkering with new ideas and projects in each of these areas, and this site is where I chronicle my work and learnings — from the smallest insights and discoveries to larger, more complex projects. If you’re interested in robotics, I hope you’ll find something valuable here. To dive deeper into my projects, research, and experiences, visit the Projects page.
